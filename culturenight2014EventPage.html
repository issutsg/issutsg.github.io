Culture Night


Last year we took you on a train ride through India's rich diversity of culture and cuisine. This year the Indian Students' Society is back to make it even bigger and better!

Join us for U of T's biggest Indian Carnival! Missing those desi melas? Fear not, because ISS is bringing that to you right on campus! There will be games, performances, food and of course lots of masti!

Do you want to play teen patti, apply henna or maybe even pin the glasses on Rajnikanth? Not to forget eat some mouthwatering Indian food, dance to those Bollywood tunes and just have some good old desi fun? Then this is the night for you!

So put on your finest attire and come out to have fun the ISS way before finals sends you into hibernation..again.

WHEN

6-8:30pm Friday, November 28 2014

WHERE

Centre for International Experience

COST

At the Door: 12$
Earlybird: $8
Earlybird group discount: 5 for 35$
