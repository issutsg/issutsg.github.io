ISS Mixer 2013

Behold, one of the most successful mixers we have ever hosted! We started the year with a bang at The Kathi Roll Express on Yonge Street in the month of September! More than 100 of our members attended and evidently by the looks of it, we all had a gala time! Our Annual Social Mixer is a way to welcome the new members of ISS as well as the new students at the University of Toronto each year. By interacting with them on a more casual and informal basis, we get to know our members better and we form amazing friendships! So gear up for our next Mixer in 2014! It's going to be even better!
