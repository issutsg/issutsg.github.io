Republic Day Debate 2013

This marked our first ever Republic Day event! In light of Republic Day on January 26th, 2013, we at ISS held a panel discussion at the University of Toronto, St. George. The topic was “The Two Indias – Is Tradition Holding Back the World's Largest Democracy?” We had an array of speakers, namely: Preeti Saran who was the current Consul General of India in Toronto, Dr. Vijaya Sripati who is a SSRHC Post-Doctoral Fellow, Mr. Sanjay Tugnait who is the Executive VP of iGate, and Srilata Raman who is an Assistant Professor in the Religion Department at the University of Toronto. The beliefs and perspectives that were shared in this discussion were varied and alot of important points about the present and the future of India as a democracy were raised. Overall, it was an enlightening discussion on the current affairs taking place in India.
