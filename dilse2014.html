Dil Se! Week 2014

Dil Se, our annual event, went bigger and better this year. Instead of just one amazing night, we had three days’ worth of festivities! Starting with Art Ki Raat, a student run art exhibition that promoted the work of students all over the GTA to Gyaan Night, which gave students the opportunity to interact with highly accomplished individuals within the U of T community and finally Dil Se where we had the famous Raghav rock the stage with hits like Angel Eyes and Ishq Shava. If that wasn’t enough, the audience also got to enjoy some mouth-watering desi khaana.
