Culture Night 2012

India, as a country, has so much to offer through its traditions, and the Indian Students’ Society tries its best to exhibit those traditions in our events. One such event where ISS expresses cultures from all the corners of Indian effortlessly is ‘Culture Night’ November 2012 was the first time we introduced the event, and it was a big success!

Culture Night – Traditions of India allowed members and friends of the ISS to enjoy traditions and activities that are performed daily, during festivals, or otherwise. These include teen patti, rangoli, Bollywood karaoke, chess, mehendi, and much more! There was plenty of food for the attendees to relish, and we ended the night with some dance! And no ISS event is complete without the ISS Blues dance team performing!
