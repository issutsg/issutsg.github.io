Scotiabank

Scotiabank is offering Indian Students’ Society students a great deal this year, so if you haven’t opened an account yet or are looking transfer accounts, check out this amazing package

Using this deal is easy!

  • Open a chequing account and a complimentary savings account with Scotiabank and get up to FIVE FREE MOVIES!
  • Get a new Scene visa card and get up to an additional FOUR FREE MOVIES!
  • Get a ONE BONUS MOVIE if you obtain both!
  • Moreover, earn Scene points every time you use your debit or credit card and you may never have to pay for a movie again!!
This offer is valid only for ISS students who visit the SPADINA – LONSDALE branch which is at 416 Spadina Road (address given in the poster). If you choose to go to another branch, you will not receive these additional movies. So visit Scotiabank at Spadina – Lonsdale, let them know you are from the Indian Students’ Society – University of Toronto and make use of this fantastic offer!

Note: You will be reimbursed with 2 TTC tokens and a Scotiabank gift if you visit the Spadina – Lonsdale branch!
