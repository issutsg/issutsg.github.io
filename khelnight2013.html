Khel Night 2013

Khel Night which was held on October 18th made a comeback because of its popular demand and it was just as heated and exciting! Along with our usual Jeopardy! formatted quiz, participants also got to polish their acting skills as we ended the evening with dumb charades, choosing between acting out a Bollywood movie and imitating an Indian cricketer.

The spirit of the game was fantastic and everybody enjoyed having their general knowledge skills put to test and devouring some spicy samosas.
